About Cat

The cat is a domesticated species of small carnivorous mammal. It is the only domesticated species in the family Felidae and is commonly referred to as the domestic cat or house cat to distinguish it from the wild members of the family.

list below are cats of different species

Abyssinian Cat

The Abyssinian is a breed of domestic short-haired cat with a distinctive "ticked" tabby coat,

in which individual hairs are banded with different colors. They are also known simply as Abys.

  • Origin: Ethiopia, South East Asia
  • Scientific name: Felis catus
  • TICA: standard

Bombay Cat

The Bombay cat is a short-haired breed of domestic cat.

Bombays are glossy solid black cats with a muscular build

and have characteristic large bright copper-golden eyes.

The breed is named after the Indian city of Bombay,

referring to the habitat of the Indian black leopard.

  • Origin: United States, Thailand
  • Scientific name: Felis catus
  • TICA: standard

Persian Cat

The Persian cat, also known as the Persian longhair,

is a long-haired breed of cat characterised by a round face and short muzzle.

The first documented ancestors of Persian cats might have been imported into Italy

from Khorasan as early as around 1620, however, this has not been proven.

  • Origin: Iran
  • Lifespan: 12 – 17 years
  • CFA: standard
  • Other names: Persian longhair, Shirazi

Things that cats like

  • Cats love grooming themselves and being groomed
  • Cats love a clean and private bathroom space
  • Cats love — and need — to scratch
  • Cats love a stimulating environment
  • Cats love high places for watching and hiding
